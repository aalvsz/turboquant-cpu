> Explain photosynthesis in one paragraph.

Photosynthesis is the remarkable process by which green plants and some other organisms convert light energy into chemical energy in the form of glucose. This process occurs within chloroplasts, organelles containing the green pigment chlorophyll, which captures light energy. Using this energy, plants combine carbon dioxide from the atmosphere with water from the soil, releasing oxygen as a byproduct. The glucose produced serves as the primary energy source for the plant's growth and other metabolic processes.

def fibonacci(n):
  """
  Computes the nth Fibonacci number.

  Args:
    n: An integer representing the desired Fibonacci number.

  Returns:
    The nth Fibonacci number.
  """
  if n <= 0:
    return 0
  elif n == 1:
    return 1
  else:
    return fibonacci(n-1) + fibonacci(n-2)
