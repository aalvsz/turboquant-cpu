## The Structure of a Haiku

A haiku is a traditional form of Japanese poetry. Its defining characteristic is its **strict structure based on syllable count**.

The structure of a haiku is:

**Three phrases (lines) with a specific syllable pattern:**

* **Line 1:** 5 syllables
* **Line 2:** 7 syllables
* **Line 3:** 5 syllables

This results in a total of **17 syllables** distributed across three lines (5-7-5).
