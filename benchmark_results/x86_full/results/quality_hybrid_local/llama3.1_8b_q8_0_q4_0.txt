> Explain photosynthesis in one paragraph.

Photosynthesis is the process by which plants, algae, and some bacteria convert light energy from the sun into chemical energy in the form of organic compounds, such as glucose. This process occurs in specialized organelles called chloroplasts, which contain the pigment chlorophyll that absorbs light energy. During photosynthesis, carbon dioxide and water are absorbed by the plant, and oxygen and glucose are produced as byproducts. The glucose produced is used by the plant as energy and building block for growth and development, while the oxygen is released into the atmosphere as a byproduct. This process is essential for life

> Write a Python function to compute the nth Fibonacci number.

def fibonacci(n):
    if n <= 0:
        return "Input should be a positive integer."
    elif n == 1:
        return 0
    elif n == 2:
        return 1
    else:
        fib_sequence = [0, 1]
        while len(fib_sequence) < n:
            fib_sequence.append(fib_sequence[-1] + fib_sequence[-2])
        return fib_sequence[-1]

# Test the function
print(fibonacci(10))  # Output: 34

F - Face: Ask the person to smile. Does one side of their face droop?

A - Arm: Ask the person to raise both arms. Does one arm drift downward?

S - Speech: Ask the person to repeat a simple sentence. Is their speech slurred or difficult to understand?

T - Time: Time is of the essence. If you notice any of these symptoms, call for emergency medical help immediately.

> Describe the structure of a haiku with an example.

A haiku is a traditional form of Japanese poetry that consists of three lines with a specific syllable count. The structure of a haiku is as follows:

* The first line has five syllables and sets the season or atmosphere for the poem.
* The second line has seven syllables and develops the idea or image introduced in the first line.
* The third line has five syllables and provides a conclusion or a final thought.

Here is an example of a haiku:

Snowflakes gently fall
Blanketing the landscape white
Winter's peaceful hush
