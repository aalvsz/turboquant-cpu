## The Structure of a Haiku

A haiku is a traditional form of Japanese poetry. Its defining characteristic is its **strict structure based on syllable count**.

The structure of a haiku is:

**Three phrases (or lines) with a specific syllable pattern: 5 - 7 - 5.**

* **Line 1:** Must have **5** syllables.
* **Line 2:** Must have **7** syllables.
* **Line 3:** Must have **5** syllables.
